**Supports**:

1. PDF Attachments - Add an IPFS of pinned attachments such as Certificates of Authenticity, Proof of Reserves, and more.
2. Unique Identifiers - Collaborate with existing working groups or make your own standard for representing unique physical items on-chain in an understandable way.
3. Item Descriptors - Define fields for capturing essential data to identify the item correctly.
4. Ownership - Permissions to update and manage an item's location and owner or custodian.
